Welcome to Merseyside 👋

Liverpool sits on the east bank of the River Mersey. Across the water is the Wirral peninsula — same metro area, different vibe, different postcodes.

  • L postcodes → Liverpool side (L1 = city centre, numbers grow outward-ish)
  • CH postcodes → Wirral side (Birkenhead, Wallasey, West Kirby, Heswall…)
  • WA / PR / WN → further out (Warrington, Southport, St Helens)
